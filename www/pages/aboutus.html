About us

Learning Space Foundation (LSF) has been working for over 5 years from 2012, along with Bombay Community Public Trust (BCPT), to spread the use of animated digital content in classroom teaching at the primary level in Zilla Parishad schools across Maharashtra.

In 2016-17, Bombay Community Public Trust (BCPT) partnered with Voltas Ltd. to focus attention on a specific use of aninmated digital content E.Teach for English language development programme in Zilla Parishad primary schools of Bhiwandi Taluka, to be implemented by LSF.

In the current year, with a State wide mandate for implementing E.Teach in primary schools, LSF has ventured further in the use of digital technology for capacity building of teachers and to better classroom teaching of English language, with a specially designed App for Zilla Parishad teachers to understand and assimilate practical use English learning components & E.Teach digital content made available by the Bombay Community Public Trust (BCPT).

The App will contain ready to use audio and video clips, games & interactive facilities for regular practice for purpose of building English language skills among vernacular primary teachers, and to improve their abilities to teach the language to village children.

The App will allow primary school teachers to access English language digital content chosen, which will be positioned for their step by step improvement of pronunciation, sentence formation, and usage of the language, especially in the context of classroom teaching. In the current year, as a pilot programme, the App will be launched for registration and regular use of primary Zilla Parishad teachers of Bhiwandi Block in Thane district.
